Welcome

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Hong Kong, focusing on Expansion and Decommissioning of New Energy Systems, Critical Metal Recycling and Supply Chain Analysis, and Climate Scenario Analysis. With a strong background in new energy science and engineering, I am passionate about developing sustainable solutions for clean energy development challenges.

My research aims to bridge the gap between environmental sustainability and technological innovation, particularly in the areas of carbon capture, solar energy systems, and circular economy solutions for renewable energy equipment.
